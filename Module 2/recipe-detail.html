Traditional Pizza, Italian home made Pasta, Cheese Cake Tart, Cheese Burger, Mutton Biryani

October 14, 2023

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 30 mins

Yields: 8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 01.

    Dough: Combine yeast, warm water, and sugar; let it sit until frothy. Mix flour and salt, add yeast mixture and olive oil, knead until elastic. Let the dough rise in a covered, oiled bowl for 1-2 hours until doubled. Pizza Sauce: 4. Sauté minced garlic in olive oil, add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens. Assembly: 6. Preheat oven to 500°F/260°C. Roll out dough, spread sauce, top with fresh mozzarella, and bake for 10-15 minutes. Optional: Add fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil before slicing. Enjoy your homemade Margherita Pizza...

  • 02.

    Combine 2 cups all-purpose flour, 3 large eggs, and a pinch of salt. Create a Mound: Make a well in the flour, crack eggs into it, and add salt. Mix and Knead: Gradually combine with a fork, then knead for 10 minutes until the dough is smooth. Rest wrapped in plastic for 30 minutes. Roll and Cut: Divide the rested dough, roll into thin sheets, and cut into desired pasta shapes. Cooking: Boil the fresh pasta in salted water for 2-4 minutes until al dente. Serve: Toss the cooked pasta with your preferred sauce, and enjoy your delightful homemade Italian pasta!

  • 03.

    Ingredients: Combine 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs with 1/3 cup melted butter. Press into tart pans and bake for 8-10 minutes. Cheesecake Filling: Beat 2 cups softened cream cheese with 1/2 cup sugar until smooth. Add eggs, vanilla extract, sour cream, and heavy cream, mixing until well combined. Assembly: Pour the cheesecake filling into the pre-baked crusts and bake for 20-25 minutes at 325°F until set at the edges but slightly jiggly in the center. Chill and Serve: Cool the tarts for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Top with fresh berries before serving. Slice and savor the creamy goodness of your homemade cheesecake tarts with the delightful crunch of the buttery crust...

  • 04.

    Ingredients: Season 1 pound of 80% lean ground beef with salt and pepper. Gather 4 hamburger buns, 4 slices of cheese, lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, and optional pickles. Have ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise for condiments. Cooking: Shape the seasoned beef into four patties and grill for 3-4 minutes per side, adding cheese in the last minute. Toast the hamburger buns while cooking. Assembly: Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each bun, top with a cooked patty, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles if desired. Condiments: Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top half of the buns. Serve: Complete the burger by placing the top half of the bun over the layered ingredients. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade cheeseburgers!

  • 05.

    Ingredients: Marinate 1 kg mutton in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, biryani masala, and salt. Soak 2 cups basmati rice and fry 2 sliced onions until golden. Cooking: Boil the rice until 70-80% cooked. In a pot, brown the marinated mutton, then layer half the rice over it. Add fried onions, tomatoes, fresh coriander, mint, and whole spices. Repeat the layers and top with saffron-infused milk. Dum Cooking: Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes for dum cooking, allowing the flavors to meld and the rice to fully cook. Serve: Gently fluff the biryani, ensuring each serving has a mix of aromatic rice and tender mutton. Serve hot and savor the rich flavors of your homemade mutton biryani!
